Xavotsav

Fine Arts

'I think most art comes out of poverty and hard times.'- Willie Nelson. But there is no 'dirth when it comes to dreams.' So if you think that you can capture the dreams and bring them to flourish on a piece of paper before you, if you believe in the eternal words of Da Vinci that,' a beautiful body perished but a work of art dies not,' then St.xavier's College Student's Council gives you an opportunity to act as a dreamcatcher on paper, and paint the pains of sacrifice and survival to make both ends meet you see and the exaltation of the small joys of life, in all colours of the spectrum or shades of black and white of blatant reality, at this year's Xavotsav. Come and experience surreality by portraying reality.

Literary Arts

A pen is mightier than a sword, they say. Use your words to freely express how the world shouldn't be divided by genders but united by the position of being human. The time to discriminate between genders is gone. Come Xavotsav, use your pen and show the world that gender equality is a dream, a dream about to come true.

Speaking Arts

Words make us.
Words break us.
But most of it all, words stay.
What you speak has the power to compel, to convince, to awaken and inspire . Be it Mark Anthony in Shakespeare's Julius Caesar or Martin Luther King Jr in the 20th century, history has been a testimony to powerful speakers whose words became the change they wanted to see.
If you think you are someone who has a sway over words that are powerful enough to brew a revolution in our hearts and minds, Xavotsav 2020 offers an arsenal of Speaking Arts events for you to choose from.
Bring out the Speaker in you then!
The stage is all yours.

Digital Arts

They said that the biggest part of evolution is changing the way we think. As society ameliorated from primitivity to modernity, art took on a new garb and humankind was engulfed by a breath of freshness that encouraged it to test it's mettle at unconventional genres.
This January, Xavotsav will let you capture hearts from behind the screens and paint them on an unusual canvas. Strengthen of your mind by harnessing technology and shifting gears to the unique world of softwares where a single click guides you to a dominion of digital treasures.

Dramatics

In today's world of equality and the most advanced developments, casteism still remains a harsh reality of Indian society. If you believe you have what it takes to make the stage your own, this year at Xavotsav, St. Xavier's College Student's Council gives you an empowering platform to make your voice heard on the prevailing discrimination that entails this social menace. This January, come and conquer the audience with your unique skills and powerful content.

Music

"Where words leave off, music begins"
- Heinrich Heine
In a country where war is catalysed by vehement haranguing, pull the strings and strike the right chords at Xavotsav, the annual fest organised by St. Xavier's College Student's Council and let the melody anchor a niche where peace prevails.

Dance

Lifestyle

"Never be bullied into silence. Never allow yourself to be made a victim. Accept no one's definition of your life; define yourself."--Harvey Fierstein
Xavotsav 2020 aims to offer a safe space to every member of the LGBTQ community. Irrespective of whether you belong to the community or you support the cause, participate in the events to bring out the beauty that hides in the 7 colours of the Pride Flag. The time to be marginalized is gone.

Sports & Gaming

Friendly rivalry, healthy competition, sabre-rattling yet fair tussle and a firm handshake in the end of all is what we call sportsman spirit, for a reason. Delve deep in the frenzy of athleticism spread over a variety of indoor and outdoor events, be the star if you emerge victorious and the sport if you fall short, all at the annual cultural fest of St. Xavier's College Student's Council, Xavotsav 2020.

Quiz

In a world where people are not fully aware of everything that happens around them, a world where people don't stop to check facts before acting out, come check your knowledge the four different quizzes organized by St. Xavier's College during Xavotsav and see how well you have an idea of the intricate workings of the world.

Journalism

The nation is torn apart by the yellow journalism and it's the need of the hour for journalists to start reporting nothing but the truth. This year at Xavotsav we aim to offer you a platform where you will be filling in the shoes of journalists and taking a shot at reporting the truth
