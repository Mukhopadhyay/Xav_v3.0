X-COMPOSE

CREATIVE WRITING

WE ALL HOLD UNFATHOMABLE STORIES INSIDE US. BUT WHEN PRESENTED WITH A PEN AND PAPER, A LOT OF US MAY DRAW A BLANK AND NOT KNOW HOW TO TELL THE WORLD WHAT WE FEEL. SO, IF YOU THINK THERE IS A POET OR AN AUTHOR INSIDE YOU WHO NEEDS JUST ONE CHANCE TO BURST FORTH ON PAPER AND PICTURE PARADISE IN WORDS, LOOK NO FURTHER. BRING FORTH ALL YOU HAVE TO SAY AND PAINT A RAINBOW OF WORDS ON THE XAVOTSAV CANVAS. THE BEST PART? WE DON’T BELIEVE IN THE VIBGYOR COLOUR CODING – WRITE WHAT YOU WANT AND IN ANY WAY YOU WANT. AT THE END OF THE DAY, THE MOST ENTHRALLING OF ALL THOSE CAREFULLY WORDED RAINBOWS SHALL TAKE HOME THE WINNER’S TROPHY!

VENUE: MAIN BUILDING (TENTATIVE)
TIME LIMIT: 75 MINUTES
NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS: 3 (1 PARTICIPANT EACH FOR ENGLISH, HINDI AND BENGALI EACH) [1 TEAM PER COLLEGE]
THEME: ON SPOT
STRUCTURE: THE TEAMS WILL BE GIVEN A TOPIC ON WHICH EACH PARTICIPANT WILL HAVE TO WRITE IN THEIR SELECTED LANGUAGE WITHIN THE PRESCRIBED TIME LIMIT.

rules:

  • EACH TEAM SHOULD CONSIST OF 3 PARTICIPANTS. ONE PARTICIPANT WILL HAVE TO WRITE IN ENGLISH, ONE PARTICIPANT WILL HAVE TO WRITE IN BENGALI AND ONE PARTICIPANT WILL HAVE TO WRITE IN HINDI.
  • EACH TEAM WILL HAVE TO WRITE IN ALL THE 3 LANGUAGES (ENGLISH, BENGALI AND HINDI)
  • EACH TEAM WILL HAVE TO HAVE 3 PARTICIPANTS, AND EACH PARTICIPANT WILL HAVE TO WRITE IN ONLY ONE LANGUAGE.
  • TEAMS WHICH FAIL TO PRODUCE 3 PARTICIPANTS, SHALL NOT BE ALLOWED TO TAKE PART IN THE EVENT.
  • PARTICIPANTS WILL BE GIVEN A THEME ON SPOT, ON THE BASIS OF WHICH THEY WILL HAVE TO WRITE A STORY.
  • THE TIME USED BY THE PARTICIPANTS IN PLANNING OR STRUCTURING THE STORYLINE IS INCLUDED WITHIN THE TIME ALLOTTED.
  • THE JUDGING WILL BE DONE, TEAM WISE, BY ADDING THE SCORES OF ALL THE 3 PARTICIPANTS OF THAT TEAM.
  • ANY USE/DISPLAY OF VULGARITY/OBSCENITY/EXPLETIVES/CONTROVERSIAL SENTIMENTS, ETC. SHALL BE PENALIZED AND SHALL LEAD TO IMMEDIATE DISQUALIFICATION.
  • ONLY TWO A4 SHEETS OF PAPER WILL BE PROVIDED PER PARTICIPANT; ALL THE OTHER NECESSARY MATERIALS ARE TO BE BROUGHT IN BY THE PARTICIPANTS THEMSELVES.

judging criteria:

  • storyline
  • originality
  • innovation
  • style of writing
  • good vocabulary
  • expression of emotions
  • wit

LEXICON

WORD GAMES

DO YOU LOVE WORDS BUT DON’T HAVE THE PATIENCE TO SIT AND PEN THEM DOWN FOR A TEDIOUS TWO HOURS? ARE YOU THE KIND OF PERSON WHO WOULD RATHER PLAY GAMES THAN SIT DOWN AND READ A BOOK? IF YES, THEN THIS IS YOUR EVENT. A PERFECT COMBINATION OF WORDS AND GAMES, THIS CAN EASILY BE CALLED ONE OF THE MOST ENJOYABLE COMPETITIONS EVER. YOU’LL HAVE A LOT OF FUN WHILE YOU’RE AT IT BUT HERE’S A TIP : BRUSH UP ON YOUR VOCABULARY SKILLS IF YOU WOULD LIKE TO BAG THE FIRST PRIZE!

VENUE: ARUPE HALL (TENTATIVE)
TIME LIMIT: NA
NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS: 2
STRUCTURE:
ROUND 1: “WHAT’S THE GOOD WORD”
THE AUDITIONS WILL CONSIST OF THREE ROUNDS. FOR THE FIRST ROUND, PARTICIPANTS WILL BE GIVEN AN ENVELOPE, EACH CONSISTING OF SCRAMBLED LETTERS THAT MAKE A WORD. THE TEAMS WILL HAVE TO PUT THE WORD TOGETHER WITHIN 2 MINUTES. THE FIRST TEAM TO DO SO WILL GET 15 POINTS, THE SECOND 10, AND THE THIRD 5. THE TEAMS UNABLE TO UNSCRAMBLE THE WORD WILL GET NO POINTS. FIVE TEAMS WILL MAKE IT TO THE SECOND ROUND. FOR THE THIRD ROUND, THE TEAMS WILL BE QUIZZED ON PALINDROMIC AND/OR PORTMANTEAU WORDS. SIMILARLY, THE FIRST TEAM TO ANSWER WITHIN 2 MINUTES WILL GET 15 MARKS, THE SECOND 10 AND THE THIRD 5. THREE TEAMS WILL MAKE IT TO THE THIRD ROUND. FOR THE THIRD ROUND, PARTICIPANTS WILL BE SHOWN CERTAIN SERIES OF PICTURES, BASED ON WHICH THEY WILL HAVE TO GUESS THE IDIOM OR PROVERB. POINTS AND TIME ALLOTTED WILL BE THE SAME AS THE PREVIOUS ROUNDS. THE TWO TEAMS WITH THE HIGHEST MARKS WILL BE SELECTED FOR THE FINALS.
ROUND 2: “TABOO”---
THERE WILL BE A TOTAL OF THREE ROUNDS. A PARTICULAR WORD WILL BE PROJECTED ON THE SCREEN WITH FOUR OTHER WORDS RELATED TO THE GIVEN WORD. (FOR INSTANCE, IF THE GIVEN WORD IS ‘PASSWORD’, THE ANCILLARY WORDS MAY BE ‘SECRET’, ‘TYPE’, ‘COMPUTER’, ‘KEY’). IN A GIVEN ROUND, ONE MEMBER FROM THE TEAM WILL FACE THE SCREEN WHILE THE OTHER WOULD FACE THE AUDIENCE. THE PARTICIPANT FACING THE SCREEN WOULD HAVE TO GET ACROSS THE GIVEN WORD TO HER/HIS TEAM MEMBER WITHOUT USING THE ANCILLARY WORDS (THE TABOO WORDS). EVEN IF THE GUESSING MEMBER OF THE TEAM HAS CORRECTLY GUESSED A TABOO WORD, THE OTHER MEMBER MUST NOT USE THE WORD HIMSELF/HERSELF TO EXPLAIN THE GIVEN WORD. EACH TEAM GETS ONE MINUTE PER TURN. EXCEPT IN THE FIRST ROUND, EACH TEAM WILL GET MULTIPLE TURNS IN EACH ROUND. THE TEAM MEMBER EXPLAINING THE WORD MAY PASS ON THE GIVEN WORD, BUT THAT WILL LEAD TO A LOSS OF 5 POINTS. IF A TEAM MEMBER, WHILE CONVEYING THE WORD, USES ANY TABOO WORD BY MISTAKE, HE/SHE WILL BE MADE AWARE OF THE MISTAKE BY THE MODERATOR AND WILL LOSE 5 POINTS. FOR EACH WORD CORRECTLY GUESSED, THE TEAM WILL BE AWARDED 10 POINTS. WHILE CONVEYING A WORD; PARTICIPANTS MUST NOT RESORT TO ABUSING, DEMEANING OR INDULGE IN MOCKERY OF ANY INDIVIDUAL. USE OF VERNACULAR LANGUAGE IS ALSO PROHIBITED. VIOLATION OF THESE RULES MIGHT LEAD TO DISQUALIFICATION. AT THE END OF 1 MINUTE ALLOTTED TO EACH TEAM, AN APPOINTED TIME KEEPER WILL RING A BELL. ANY WORD GUESSED AFTER THIS TIME PERIOD WILL NOT BE COUNTED.

rules:

  • IF THERE IS A TIE BETWEEN TWO TEAMS AT THE END OF ANY ROUND THERE SHALL BE A SURPRISE TIE BREAKER ROUND.
  • N CASE OF ANY CONFUSION, THE DECISION OF THE MODERATOR WILL BE FINAL. THE DECISION OF THE MODERATOR IS NOT OPEN TO ANY FORM OF DEBATE.
  • TEAMS ARE REQUESTED TO NOT SHOUT OUT THE ANSWERS OR ASSIST OTHER TEAMS DURING THE EVENT. TEAMS FOUND DOING SO SHALL BE HEAVILY PENALIZED AND SUBJECTED TO IMMEDIATE DISQUALIFICATION.
  • THE TIME LIMIT OF EVERY ROUND WILL BE INFORMED PRIOR TO THE COMMENCEMENT OF THE EVENT AND SHOULD BE STRICTLY ADHERED TO BY THE PARTICIPANTS.

X-PLAIN

BOOK CHARADES

WE ALL HAVE THOSE MOMENTS OF JOY, WHEN WE USE CODES AND SIGNALS TO EXPRESS A JOKE TO OUR BEST FRIEND BEHIND OUR TEACHERS ONLY TO BURST INTO SILENT FITS OF LAUGHTER. WANT TO RELIVE THOSE MOMENTS? RELIVE THEM WHILE ACTING OUT THE PHRASE TO YOUR FRIENDS, AT BOOK CHARADES! GIRLS AND BOYS COME TOGETHER AND GET READY TO SHOW OFF YOUR WIT AND EXPLAIN TO YOUR GANG, ALL THE SAD AND HAPPY WORDS THAT ARE PRESENT IN THE PHRASE. ENJOY YOURSELF BECAUSE THERE ARE NO STUPID WAYS IN THE GAME OF BOOK OF CHARADES. USE OUR BODY AND MIND WELL TO GIVE YOUR FRIENDS A PICTURE OF THE PHRASE AND THEREBY EARN A STEP TOWARDS YOUR PARADISE WHERE LIES YOUR TROPHY!

VENUE: TO BE ANNOUNCED
TIME LIMIT: 2 MINUTES
NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS: 4
STRUCTURE:
EACH ROUND OF THE GAME PROCEEDS AS FOLLOWS:

  • A PLAYER FROM TEAM A DRAWS A SLIP FROM A COMMON BOWL. AFTER HE/SHE HAS HAD A TIME SPAN OF 10 SECONDS TO REVIEW IT, THE TIMEKEEPER ASKS THE PLAYER TO START. TEAM A HAS 2 MINUTES TO GUESS THE NAME OF THE BOOK. IF THE TEAM FIGURES IT OUT, THE TIME KEEPER WRITES DOWN THE TIME. IF THEY CAN’T THEM THE TIMEKEEPER WRITES DOWN 2 MINUTES. MARKS WILL BE AWARDED TO THE TEAM THAT FINISHES THE FASTEST.
  • A PLAYER FROM TEAM B THEN DRAWS A SLIP AND THE GAME PROCEEDS AS MENTIONED ABOVE.
EACH ROUND ENDS WITH ALL TEAMS ACTING AT ONCE.THE TEAM WITH THE HIGHEST SCORE WINS THE ROUND.

rules:

  • ANY TEAM CAUGHT USING UNFAIR MEANS AND/OR ASSISTING OTHER TEAMS DURING THE CONTEST WILL FACE DISQUALIFICATION.
  • ONLY ONE TEAM OF 4 MEMBERS CAN BE SENT FROM EACH COLLEGE
  • PARTICIPANTS ARE ADVISED TO HAVE AN IDEA OF THE GESTURES USED IN CHARADES AND A FAIR IDEA OF DIFFERENT CATEGORIES OF BOOKS.
  • ORGANIZERS RESERVE THE RIGHT TO MODIFY THE RULES OF THE EVENT AT ANY TIME. CONTESTANTS WILL BE DULY INFORMED.
  • THERE WILL BE FIVE ROUNDS WITH ELIMINATION AFTER EACH ROUND.
  • IF A TEAM IS UNABLE TO GUESS THE NAME, THE OTHER TEAMS WILL BE GIVEN AN OPPORTUNITY TO GUESS IT AND BONUS POINTS WILL BE AWARDED.

BLACKOUT POETRY

DESPITE THE GALLANT EFFORTS, THERE ALWAYS COMES A TIME WHEN OUR BRAND NEW BOOK WITH THE LOVELY BOOKISH SMELL, FALLS APART ONLY TO BE RENDERED USELESS. DO YOU BELONG TO THAT GROUP OF PEOPLE WHO WOULD STILL DO SOMETHING CREATIVE WITH YOUR ONCE TREASURED BOOK? WELL THEN, YOU ARE WELCOME TO THE GAME OF BLACKOUT POETRY. HERE YOU GET YOUR CHANCE TO PRESENT A PICTURE OF YOUR PARADISE, EMBEDDED WITH A CREATIVE POEM. COME TOGETHER TO REPURPOSE AND HONOR THE MEMORY OF THE OLD WORN OUT BOOK PAGE. CIRCLE THE WORDS THAT YOU WILL USE IN THE POEM AND THEN USE THE REST OF THE PAGE TO GIVE TO THE READERS A WONDERFUL PICTURE OF THE PARADISE THAT ONLY YOU CAN SEE IN YOUR MIND!
