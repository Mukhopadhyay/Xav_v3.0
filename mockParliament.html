"Political democracy cannot last unless there lies at the base of it social democracy. What does social democracy mean? It means a way of life which recognizes liberty, equality and fraternity as the principles of life."
_–B R Ambedkar_
The political fabric of the parliamentary democracy of India paves the way to articulate one's standpoint in the most unprecedented manner. An assemblage of 448 articles and 12 schedules—the Indian Constitution forms the basis of such a discourse.
*Xavotsav 2020 presents to you the 'Mock Parliament' Competition*, a simulation of the Lower House of the Parliament. The participants are required to represent important Members of the Parliament and engage in an intellectual discussion by keeping in mind the policies of their affiliated political parties.
*The Lok Sabha*, as it is called, is vested with special provisions which makes it more powerful than its bicameral counterpart in the Indian Parliament. *The Mock Parliament* not only serves as an educational exercise for all its participants, but also provides a platform to put forward the change in the current political scenario that they would like to experience in the years to follow.
Join us in the house to experience euphoria like never before.

Dates: 22, 23 January, 2020
Time: 10:00am - 2:00pm both days
Participation type: Single delegation. (one person representing one portfolio).
Agenda: A roadmap towards drafting a national immigration policy for india.
rules of procedure: lok sabha parliamentary procedures.
