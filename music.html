TIME LIMIT: NA
NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS: 1
STRUCTURE (FOR XTACY AND XAVOTSAV):
ROUND 1 (INTRODUCTORY ROUND): EVERY PARTICIPANT MUST INTRODUCE HIMSELF/HERSELF TO THE JUDGES AND MUST SING A SONG OF HIS/HER CHOICE. THE DURATION OF EACH SONG SHALL BE SUBJECT TO THE JUDGE’S DISCRETION.
ROUND 2 (ME AND YOU ROUND): EVERY PARTICIPANT WILL BE RANDOMLY PAIRED WITH ANOTHER PARTICIPANT AND BOTH OF THEM WILL SING TOGETHER. THERE ARE NO RESTRICTIONS AS TO THE CHOICE OF THE SONG FOR THE DUET PERFORMANCE. AGAIN, THE DURATION OF THE DUET WILL BE SUBJECT TO THE JUDGE’S DISCRETION.
ROUND 3 (MEDLEY ROUND): EACH PARTICIPANT OF ROUND 3 WILL HAVE TO SING A MEDLEY OF ATLEAST 3 SONGS, THE DURATION OF WHICH SHALL BE DECIDED BY THE JUDGE.
ROUND 4 (FACE OFF): THE BEST TWO SINGERS FROM ROUND 3 WILL COMPETE IN A FACE OFF AGAINST EACH OTHER. THE LAST VOCALIST STANDING EARNS THE TITLE OF NIGHTINGALE OF THE PARADISE.

rules:

  • THE LANGUAGES OF THE SONGS CAN BE HINDI/ENGLISH/BENGALI.
  • NO PRE-RECORDED TRACKS OR AN ACCOMPANIST WILL BE ALLOWED.
  • OBSCENITY OF ANY KIND IS NOT ALLOWED AND WILL LEAD TO IMMEDIATE DISQUALIFICATION.

judging criteria:

  • quality of vocals
  • choice of songs
  • stage presence
  • crown appeal
  • personality and confidence
  • overall impact
