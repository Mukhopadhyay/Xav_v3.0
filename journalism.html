Bollywood Group dance

Ever since it's wee beginnings, if there's one thing that bollywood has excelled at, it has been its multitudes of shades and hues. Be it in costumes or in movements, watching them on screen has always been an absolute pleasure.   • PRE-RECORDED MUSIC SHALL BE BROUGHT IN A PEN- DRIVE IN .MP3 FORMAT ONLY (AS OTHER FORMATS MAY NOT BE SUPPORTED IN THE COMPUTER – USE THEM AT YOUR OWN RISK).
  • PARTICIPANTS MUST STRICTLY ADHERE TO THE TIME LIMIT.
  • ONLY ONE TEAM PER COLLEGE IS ALLOWED
  • PERFORMERS HAVE TO CLEAR THE STAGE AFTER THEIR PERFORMANCE OTHERWISE, THEY WILL BE PENALIZED HEAVILY BY THE ORGANIZERS.
  • ANY KIND OF FLUID POWDER FLAME OR HEAVY OBJECT IS NOT ALLOWED ON STAGE.
  • OBSCENITY OF ANY KIND IS NOT ALLOWED AND WILL LEAD TO IMMEDIATE DISQUALIFICATION.
  • THE DECISIONS OF THE JUDGES SHALL BE FINAL AND BINDING.
  • A MINIMUM OF 6 MEMBERS OF THE TEAM SHOULD BE PRESENT ON STAGE ALL THE TIME DURING THE PERFORMANCE.

  • Choreography
  • usage of costumes and props
  • choice of proper music
  • audience response
  • synchronization
  • Implementation of theme
  • Innovation
