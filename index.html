Official Aftermovie

XAVOTSAV is the annual cultural extravaganza organized by the St. Xavier’s College Students’ Council. It has events spread across various spectrum’s such as performing arts, fine arts, literature, lifestyle and sports. Year after year our resolve to make Xavotsav bigger and better only strengthens. The likes of The Raghu Dixit Project, Nucleya, Papon and East India Company, Euphoria, Hard Kaur, Lost Stories, Agnee, Shirley Setia, Marnkik, and Sevenn have set the stage on fire in the past.
