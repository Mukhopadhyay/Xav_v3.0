ON THE SPOT

EXTEMPORE

IF THOUGHTS COME INSTANTANEOUSLY TO YOUR MIND, AND YOU DON'T FEEL THE NEED OF PROLONGED OVER-THINKING TO DELIVER, THEN THIS IS THE EVENT YOU SHOULD LOOK OUT FOR. IDEATE, SPEAK AND IMPRESS ALL IN 1 MINUTE AND CARVE YOUR OWN NICHE WHEN IT COMES TO THE SPOKEN WORD. YOU'VE GOT A MINUTE TO WIN IT!

VENUE: STREET STAGE
TIME LIMIT: 1 MINUTE
PARTICIPANTS: 1
STRUCTURE:
EACH PARTICIPANT HAS TO SPEAK IN CLEAR AND FLUENT SPEECH FOR EXACTLY ONE MINUTE ON THE GIVEN TOPIC.

rules:

  • RULES ARE STRICTLY SUBJECT TO JUDGES’ PRIORITY, SAVE A FEW BASIC TENETS.
  • PARTICIPANTS MUST BE CLEAR, FLUENT AND ENTERTAINING.
  • USE OF QUEEN’S ENGLISH IS A MUST.

judging criteria

  • spontaneity
  • wit
  • humor
  • miscellaneous (other criteria completely subject to judges' discretion)

X-VERSE

POETRY SLAM

A HUGE CHUNK OF THE MAGIC IN A STORY LIES IN HOW YOU PRODUCE IT. WE CHANCED UPON A SPECIAL BREED OF PEOPLE WHO ARE AMAZING STORYTELLERS. BUT THEY CAN WEAVE THEM IN VERSES. IF YOU THINK YOU FALL UNDER SUCH CATEGORY, FRET NO MORE, BECAUSE HERE'S AN OPPORTUNITY FOR YOU TO BRING OUT THE MAGICAL SHADES OF YOUR POETRY! YES, YOU HEARD THAT RIGHT. POETRY HAS MANY LAYERS IN IT AND YOU'LL DECIDE WHICH HUE OF YOUR POEM YOU’D WANT TO SHOWCASE. ONE MOMENT YOU’RE IN A SERENE SPACE OF PARADISE, AND ON THE VERY NEXT, YOU'RE ON THE XAVOTSAV STAGE.

VENUE: FR. DEPELCHIN AUDITORIUM (TENTATIVE)
TIME LIMIT: 3+1 MINUTES
NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS: 1
STRUCTURE:
EACH PARTICIPANT HAS TO RECITE OR ACT OUT HIS/HER SELF-WRITTEN POEM ON STAGE WITHIN THE GIVEN TIME LIMIT.

rules:

  • THE POEMS MUST BE SELF-WRITTEN, PLAGIARISM WILL NOT BE ENTERTAINED.
  • THE POEMS CAN BE WRITTEN IN ENGLISH, HINDI OR BENGALI.
  • EACH POET GETS THREE MINUTES, PLUS A TEN-SECOND GRACE PERIOD, TO READ ONE POEM. IF THE POET GOES OVER TIME, POINTS WILL BE DEDUCTED FROM THE TOTAL SCORE.
  • NO PROPS, COSTUMES CAN BE USED BY THE POET.
  • MAXIMUM 2 SAMPLINGS, QUOTES OR REFERENCES IN ONE POEM.
  • MUST RELATE TO THE THEME.
  • THE AUTHOR OF THE CONTENT SHOULD BE THE ONE PERFORMING THE PIECE.
  • ACOUSTIC MUSICAL INSTRUMENTS ARE ALLOWED.
  • MUST NOT BE SEXIST, RACIST, HOMOPHOBIC, TRANSPHOBIC, CISSEXIST OR PERPETUATE/PROPAGATE HATE TOWARDS ANY MINORITY OR TARGET ANY RACE, RELIGION AND ETHNICITY OTHERWISE.
  • PERFORMANCES THAT EXCEED THE TIME LIMIT WILL BE PENALIZED.
  • ONLY ONE PARTICIPANT ALLOWED PER COLLEGE.
  • OBSCENITY/VULGARITY OF ANY KIND IS NOT ALLOWED AND SUBJECT TO IMMEDIATE DISQUALIFICATION.

judging criteria:

  • performance (delivery and impact)
  • content (originality and composition)
  • timing

STAND UP COMEDY

NOT EVERYONE CAN TICKLE THE FUNNY BONE, THAT TOO, IN FRONT OF A CROWD. IF YOU THINK YOU CAN MAKE PEOPLE CRACK UP WITH YOUR JOKES AND ELICIT APPLAUSE FOR YOUR PERFORMANCE, THEN THIS EVENT IS TAILOR-MADE FOR YOU. IN THE MIDST OF THE BOOMING NATIONAL STAND-UP SCENE, IT IS PRUDENT THAT XAVOTSAV OFFERS A PLATFORM FOR BUDDING COMEDIANS. EXPLOIT THE LOOPHOLES IN NORMATIVE SITUATIONS, EXTRICATE THE HUMOUR FROM THE MUNDANE AND START A LAUGH RIOT.

VENUE: STREET STAGE (TENTATIVE)
TIME LIMIT: 3+1 MINUTE (INCLUDING SETUP AND CLEARANCE TIME)
PARTICIPANTS: 1
STRUCTURE:
EACH PARTICIPANT HAS TO PRESENT A COMIC ACT WITHIN THE PRESCRIBED TIME LIMIT, USING PROPS IF NEEDED.

rules:

  • MONO-ACTING, MUSICALS AND PUPPETRY ARE NOT ALLOWED.
  • USE OF PROPS IS ALLOWED AND THEY ARE TO BE BROUGHT BY THE PARTICIPANT THEMSELVES. THE ORGANISERS HOLDS THE DISCRETION OF ALLOWING IT ON STAGE.
  • USE OF OBSCENITY/PROFANITY IS NOT ALLOWED (AS LONG AS THERE ARE NO DIRECT IMPLICATIONS) AND WILL BE SUBJECT TO IMMEDIATE DISQUALIFICATION.
  • THE JUDGE’S DECISION SHALL BE FINAL AND BINDING.

judging criteria

  • wit
  • humor
  • content
  • presentation
  • originality
  • stage presentation
  • audienc interaction
  • wiseness

WAR OF WORDS

ENGLISH DEBATE

WHEN THE PROVERBIAL 'UNSTOPPABLE FORCE MEETS AN IMMOVABLE OBJECT', THE INTELLECTUAL DEBATE THICKENS. IF YOU CONSIDER YOUR RATIONALE TO BE MANIPULATIVE AND CONVINCING, THEN THE ENGLISH DEBATE IS WHAT YOU SHOULD SIGN UP FOR. REASON OUT WITHIN A FRAMEWORK; EXPEND YOUR ORATORICAL SKILLS, RHETORIC, LOGICAL THINKING AND CAST A SPELL WITH THE SPOKEN WORD.

VENUE: ARUPE HALL (TENTATIVE)
TIME LIMIT: 3+1 MINUTES
PARTICIPANTS: 1
STRUCTURE:
EACH PARTICIPANT HAS TO GIVE A SPEECH ON THE GIVEN MOTION, WHETHER FOR OR AGAINST AND RESPOND TO THE REBUTTAL QUESTIONS WITHIN THE PRESCRIBED TIME LIMIT.

rules:

  • THE MOTION WILL BE DECLARED IN ADVANCE TO THE COLLEGE REPRESENTATIVES, WHO ARE THEN REQUIRED TO COMMUNICATE IT TO THE PARTICIPANTS OF THEIR RESPECTIVE COLLEGES.
  • THERE WILL BE 8 TEAMS IN TOTAL.
  • EACH TEAM SHALL CONSIST OF EXACTLY 2 SPEAKERS- ONE SPEAKING FOR THE MOTION AND THE OTHER SPEAKING AGAINST THE MOTION.
  • EACH SPEAKER WILL BE GIVEN 3+1 MINUTES TO SPEAK, AFTER WHICH HE/SHE WILL BE GIVEN 2 MINUTES FOR REBUTTAL. THE JUDGES MAY QUESTION THE SPEAKER IN THE EVENT THAT NONE OF THE COMPETING TEAMS ARE ASKED ANY QUESTIONS DURING THE REBUTTAL ROUND.
  • A WARNING BELL SHALL BE RUNG AT THE 3RD MINUTE FOR ALL PARTICIPANTS.
  • USE OF NON-PARLIAMENTARY LANGUAGE IS PROHIBITED.
  • CROSS QUESTIONING IS NOT ALLOWED.
  • THE PARTICIPANTS WILL NOT BE ALLOWED TO READ OUT FROM ANY MATERIAL, BUT WILL BE ALLOWED TO CARRY CHITS FOR REFERENCE.
  • EXCEEDING OR FINISHING VERY EARLY TO THE LIMIT GIVEN AND PERSONAL ATTACKS ON THEE SPEAKERS WILL LEAD TO PENALIZATION AS PER THE DISCRETION OF THE JUDGES.
  • JUDGING CRITERIA: CONTENT (40%), DELIVERY (40%) AND REBUTTAL (20%). IF NO REBUTTAL QUESTIONS ARE ASKED BY THE PARTICIPANTS, THE SPEAKER SHALL BE AWARDED FULL MARKS.
  • THE DECISION OF THE JUDGES IS FINAL.
  • THE ORGANIZING TEAM RESERVES THE TO CHANGE OR MODIFY ANY OF THE RULES.

judging criteria:

  • content
  • delivery
  • rebuttal
  • speaking skills
  • fluency

PASS THE TORCH

RELAY STORYTELLING

HAS A SMALL INCIDENT EVER SPARKED OFF AN AMALGAMATION OF THOUGHTS? CHAIN OF ACTIONS AND REACTIONS IN YOUR MIND? WELL, THE COSMOS OF STORIES DEMAND YOUR ATTENTION THERE. INITIATION POINTS COULD BE MANY, BUT WHAT'S YOUR PICK? THINK, WEAVE AND PASS ON TO EXECUTE YOUR OWN UNIQUE TALE. OF COURSE, YOU'RE NOT ALONE IN THIS. THE THREE MINDS COME TOGETHER TO ARTICULATE THE TALES OF JOY OR SORROW, A FALL OR RISE OR MAYBE A PICTURE OF PARADISE!

VENUE: FR. DEPELCHIN AUDITORIUM (TENTATIVE)
TIME LIMIT: 5+1 MINUTES
NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS: 3
STRUCTURE:
A TEAM HAS TO CONCOCT A STORY ON-STAGE BASED ON THE GIVEN THEME WITHIN THE PRESCRIBED TIME LIMIT OF 5+1 MINUTES.

rules:

  • EACH INSTITUTION CAN SEND A MAXIMUM OF ONE TEAM, WHICH MUST COMPRISE OF THREE INDIVIDUALS FOR THIS PARTICULAR. EVENT. A GROUP MEMBER FROM EACH TEAM WILL HAVE TO COME UP 10 MINUTES PRIOR TO THE EVENT AND PICK A CHIT.
  • THE CHITS WILL HAVE THE TOPIC MENTIONED WHICH CAN RANGE FROM A STARTING LINE TO JUST A PHRASE OR PERHAPS, JUST A WORD.
  • EACH TEAM WILL BE ALLOTTED PRECISELY THREE MINUTES TO CONCOCT A STORY ON-STAGE.
  • AFTER THE FIRST TEAM MEMBER BEGINS, A BELL SHALL BE SOUNDED AFTER ONE MINUTE, FOLLOWING WHICH THE SECOND MEMBER MUST CONTINUE THE STORY FOR THE NEXT MINUTE BEFORE THE THIRD MEMBER WRAPS UP THE STORY IN THE REMAINING MINUTE. BELLS WILL BE SOUNDED TO INDICATE THE PASSAGE OF TIME THAT SIGNALS THE CHANGE OF TEAM MEMBERS REQUIRED TO CONTINUE WITH THE STORY.
  • EXTRA TIME USED BY THE TEAMS WILL BE NOTED AND CONSIDERED NEGATIVELY IN THE JUDGEMENT PROCESS.
  • PARTICIPANTS WILL BE JUDGED ON THE BASIS OF THEIR CREATIVITY, COHERENCE, AND TEAMWORK.
  • THE DECISION OF THE JUDGE(S) SHALL BE FINAL AND BINDING.

judging criteria:

  • performance (delivery and impact)
  • content (originality and composition)
  • timing

CHANNEL-EYES

CHANNEL SURFING

A FUN EVENT, THIS WILL BRING OUT THE QUIRKY AND OUT-OF-THE-BOX TALENT OF OUR PARTICIPANTS. CAN YOU SPEAK THE LINGO OF TV CHANNELS AS FAST AS YOU SURF THEM? ONE OF THE NEW ADDITIONS OF XAVOTSAV EVENT ROSTER, THIS EVENT PROMISES TO BE AN ENTERTAINING AFFAIR, WITH A LOT OF TONGUE TWISTING! THINK YOU CAN DO THE JOB OF IDIOT BOX AS EASILY? WE’LL SEE, THAT TOO IN HIGH DEFINITION.

VENUE: ROOM 11 (TENTATIVE)
TIME LIMIT: 3+1 MINUTES (INCLUDING SETUP AND CLEARANCE TIME)
PARTICIPANTS: 1
STRUCTURE:
PARTICIPANTS ARE TO PERFORM FOR A PERIOD OF 3+1 MINUTES INCLUDING DIALOGUES OF ANY 5 TV CHANNELS OF THEIR CHOICE. CHANGE TO THE NEXT CHANNEL MUST BE MADE WHEN THE JUDGE DECIDES TO CHANGE THE CHANNEL. 8 PARTICIPANTS WILL BE SELECTED FOR THE FINAL ROUND.

rules:

  • PARTICIPANTS ARE ADVISED TO PREPARE THEMSELVES AS PER THE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS EVENT.
  • CHOICE OF CHANNELS FOR THE PRELIMS MUST BE COMMUNICATED TO THE ORGANISERS BEFOREHAND.
  • CHANNELS MAY VARY FROM THE NEWS, SPORTS, MOVIES AND ENTERTAINMENT CHANNELS BUT SHOULD NOT INCLUDE ANY REGIONAL CHANNELS.
  • MEDIUM OF CHANNELS WILL BE IN HINDI AND ENGLISH ONLY.
  • OBSCENITY IS STRICTLY PROHIBITED AND ANY DEVIATION AS SUCH WILL LEAD TO DISQUALIFICATION.
  • DECISION OF THE JUDGES SHALL BE FINAL AND BINDING.
  • ORGANIZERS RESERVE THE RIGHT TO MODIFY THE RULES OF THE EVENT ANYTIME.

judging criteria:

  • wit and humor
  • transition
  • clarity
  • performance and impact
  • relevance to channel
  • any criteria and judges deem fit
