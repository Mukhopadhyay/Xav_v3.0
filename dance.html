RIP-OFF

Street battle

WE AT XAVOTSAV BELIEVE THAT THERE CAN BE NO ONE WAY TO EXPRESS YOURSELF. SO IF YOU DON’T FIT THE STEREOTYPED CATEGORIES BUT LOVE TO DANCE ALL THE SAME, THEN THIS IS YOUR EVENT. COME BE A PART OF THIS BATTLE – LET LOOSE THOSE MYRIAD HUES INSIDE OF YOU AND LET GO OF ALL BLUES. ENTHRAL OUR AUDIENCE WITH THE PAINTINGS THAT YOUR MOVEMENTS TRACE.

TIME LIMIT: NA
NO. OF PARTICIPANTS: 1 PER TEAM/COLLEGE
STRUCTURE:

KNOCKOUT ROUND – AT RANDOM TWO PARTICIPANTS WILL BE CALLED ON STAGE AND ASKED TO BATTLE EACH OTHER ONE ON ONE ON A CONTINUING MUSIC TRACK. THIS KEEPS ON GOING IN A KNOCKOUT FORMAT TILL HALF OF THE TOTAL NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS HAVE BEEN ELIMINATED. FINAL ROUND: THE REMAINING PARTICIPANTS WILL HAVE TO PERFORM IN A GROUP FORMAT ON A SELECTED TRACK, AND THE BEST ONE WINS.

RULES:

  • USAGE OF PROPS IS ALLOWED. THESE ARE TO BE BROUGHT BY THE TEAMS THEMSELVES. THE ORGANIZERS HOLD THE DISCRETION TO ALLOW THE PROP ON STAGE.
  • TEAMS ARE REQUESTED TO SUBMIT THE PROP LIST PRIOR TO THE COMPETITION. THE ORGANIZERS SHALL NOT BEAR ANY RESPONSIBILITY IN CASE OF LOSS OR DAMAGE OF PROP MATERIALS.
  • PARTICIPANTS ARE NOT ALLOWED TO BRING IN ANY FORM OR PRE-RECORDED MUSIC OR THEIR OWN TRACKS. ONLY ONE TEAM PER COLLEGE IS ALLOWED
  • PERFORMERS HAVE TO CLEAR THE STAGE AFTER THEIR PERFORMANCE OTHERWISE THEY WILL BE PENALIZED HEAVILY BY THE ORGANIZERS.
  • ANY KIND OF FLUID, POWDER, FLAME OR HEAVY OBJECT IS NOT ALLOWED ON STAGE.
  • OBSCENITY OF ANY KIND IS NOT ALLOWED AND WILL LEAD TO IMMEDIATE DISQUALIFICATION
  • THE DECISION OF THE JUDGES SHALL BE FINAL AND BINDING.
  • TEAMS MUST PERFORM A PART OF THE SEQUENCE THAT THEY INTEND TO PERFORM IN THE FINALS IN THE ELIMINATION ROUND.
  • TEAMS MUST PERFORM AN EXTENDED VERSION OF THE SEQUENCE PERFORMED DURING THE ELIMS IN THE FINAL ROUND.

juding criteria:

  • spontaneity
  • usage of costume and props
  • audience response
  • expression
  • innovation
  • creativity
  • coordination
  • reflex
