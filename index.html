About Us

St. Xavier’s College (Autonomous), Kolkata has been a platform for the finest talents and brightest minds of the country. The Xaverian spirit of ‘Nihil Ultra’ or Nothing Beyond has been a guiding force behind every member of the Xaverian family. This guiding force has always inspired Xaverians to explore the riches in a world of limitless possibilities and thus we aim to create and enter newer avenues of excellence with ‘XAVOTSAV 2019’ to be held on the 22nd, 23rd and 24th of January, 2019. Official Aftermovie

XAVOTSAV is the annual cultural extravaganza organized by the St. Xavier’s College Students’ Council. It has events spread across various spectrum’s such as performing arts, fine arts, literature, lifestyle and sports. Year after year our resolve to make Xavotsav bigger and better only strengthens. The likes of The Raghu Dixit Project, Nucleya, Papon and East India Company, Euphoria, Hard Kaur, Lost Stories, Agnee, Shirley Setia, Marnkik, and Sevenn have set the stage on fire in the past.
