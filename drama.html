STAGE PLAY

THE STAGE IS SET. LIGHTS ON. THE AUDIENCE HAS TAKEN THEIR SEATS, WAITING WITH BAITED BREATH. DO YOU HAVE WHAT IT TAKES TO TRANSFIX AND MESMERIZE THEM? STAGE PLAY IS THE EVENT WHERE YOU BRING OUT THE FLAIR FOR THEATRE INSIDE YOU, AND IGNITE THE PROSCENIUM WITH YOUR NONPAREIL EXHIBITION. CHANNELIZE THE COLLECTIVE ACUMEN OF EVERYONE INVOLVED IN A THEATRE TROUPE, AND PRODUCE A PLAY WHICH WILL HOLD EVERY WITNESS TO THRALL. DRAMATIZE WITH DIGNITY AND CATER TO THE SENSIBILITIES OF THE AUDIENCE.

THEME: TO BE INFORMED TO CONTINGENT LEADER.

NUKKAD NATAK

STREET PLAY

HARK BACK TO THE DAYS OF THE REVOLUTIONARY IPTA. WHEN BADAL SIRCAR TOOK THEATRE OUT OF THE PROSCENIUM AND INTO THE STREETS, IT WAS IN ORDER TO FOSTER A GREATER GRASSROOT CONNECT. IN THIS EVENT, WE ASK YOU TO THINK OF AN ISSUE PLAGUING THE SOCIETY, AND PRODUCE A STREET PLAY WHICH WILL DRAMATICALLY TREAT THE ISSUE. EMPLOY STIMULATING METHODS TO AROUSE EMOTIONS, ACT AND SING BOISTEROUSLY AND UP THE ANTE. GO THE EXTRA MILE, TRANSCEND THE BARRIERS OF THE STAGE, PUT THE PEDAL TO THE METAL AND LEAVE THE AUDIENCE BAFFLED.

NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS: 8-14 (INCLUSIVE OF MUSICIANS) (2 CAS SHALL BE ALLOWED)
THEME: ANY RELEVANT SOCIAL CAUSE
STRUCTURE:
XTACY: THE PARTICIPANTS HAVE TO PERFORM A STREET PLAY WITHIN THE PRESCRIBED TIME LIMIT BASED ON ANY RELEVANT SOCIAL CAUSE (5+1 MINUTES [INCLUDING SETUP AD CLEARANCE TIME]). IT SHOULD BE A TRAILER OF THEIR FINAL PERFORMANCE.
XAVOTSAV: THE PARTICIPANTS HAVE TO PERFORM A STREET PLAY WITHIN THE PRESCRIBED TIME LIMIT BASED ON ANY RELEVANT SOCIAL CAUSE (9+1 MINUTES [INCLUDING SETUP AD CLEARANCE TIME]) EXTENDED VERSION OF THE TRAILER PERFORMED IN XTACY.

rules:

  • THE TEAM SIZE REPRESENTS THE NUMBER OF PEOPLE REGISTERED AS A TEAM. ONLY THOSE SHALL BE ALLOWED TO PERFORM THE STREET PLAY.
  • the language should be english, hindi or bengali
  • a minimum of 8 members should be included in the team.
  • script should be original and should not be copied. if found guilty, would result in disqualification of the team.
  • teams will be penalised if they cross the prescribed time limit.
  • NO MICS OR PLUG-INS (FOR INSTRUMENTS) SHALL BE PROVIDED. NO PRE-RECORDED MUSIC SHALL BE ALLOWED. ONLY LIVE MUSIC IS ALLOWED.
  • the devision of the judges shall be final and binding
  • only one team per college is allowed.
  • PERFORMERS HAVE TO CLEAR THE GROUND AFTER THEIR PERFORMANCE OTHERWISE THEY WILL BE PENALISED HEAVILY BY THE ORGANISERS.
  • ANY KIND OF FLUID, POWDER, FLAME OR HEAVY OBJECT IS NOT ALLOWED.
  • TEAMS MUST PERFORM A PART OF THE SEQUENCE THAT THEY INTEND TO PERFORM IN THE FINALS IN THE ROUND OF ELIMINATION.
  • TEAMS MUST PERFORM AN EXTENDED VERSION OF THE SEQUENCE PERFORMED DURING XTACY IN XAVOTSAV.
  • ONLY HOUSEHOLD OBJECTS TO BE USED AS PROPS (DHOLAK, STICK, DRUM, GULAAL ARE ALLOWED). OTHER PROPS IS ALLOWED. THESE ARE TO BE BROUGHT BY THE TEAMS THEMSELVES. THE ORGANISERS HOLD THE DISCRETION OF ALLOWING IT ON GROUND.

CHARACTER REPRESENTATION

IMPERSONATION CAN BE THE HIGHEST FORM OF FLATTERY. IMPERSONATION A.K.A IMPRESSION REQUIRES GREAT OBSERVATION SKILLS, WHICH DO NOT COME EASY. IF YOU CONSIDER YOURSELF SOMEONE WHO CAN TALK, WALK OR BEHAVE EXACTLY LIKE SOMEONE ELSE, SUCH THAT YOU CAN PERFECTLY FIT THAT PERSON'S SHOES, THEN SHOW US WHAT YOU GOT! SHOWCASE WHAT COMES TO YOU ORGANICALLY, AND AMAZE THE CROWD BY BEING THE OTHER 'YOU'.

NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS: 1-3 (1 CAS SHALL BE ALLOWED)
THEME: WILL BE INFORMED TO THE CONTINGENT LEADER
STRUCTURE:
XTACY: THE PARTICIPANT(S) HAS TO PERFORM AN ACT OF ANY POPULAR PERSONALITY/CHARACTER WITHIN THE PRESCRIBED TIME LIMIT (2+1 MINUTES [INCLUDING SETUP AD CLEARANCE TIME]). IT SHOULD BE A TRAILER OF THEIR FINAL PERFORMANCE.
XAVOTSAV: THE PARTICIPANT(S) HAVE TO PERFORM AN ACT OF ANY POPULAR PERSONALITY/CHARACTER WITHIN THE PRESCRIBED TIME LIMIT BASED ON ANY RELEVANT SOCIAL CAUSE (5+2 MINUTES [INCLUDING SETUP AD CLEARANCE TIME]) EXTENDED VERSION OF THE TRAILER PERFORMED IN XTACY.

rules:

  1. THE CHARACTER REPRESENTATION CAN BE PORTRAYED BY THE TEAMS THROUGH THEIR OWN ANALYSIS OF ANY CHARACTER. PARTICIPANTS CAN PRESENT THEIR OWN VERSION OF THE CHARACTERS.
  2. PARTICIPANTS MUST NOT EXCEED THE TIME LIMIT OF 5(+2) MINUTES.
  3. THEY WILL BE MARKED ON THE BASIS OF CREATIVE INPUTS,CONTENT,CHARACTER FORMATION,DELIVERY,SENSE OF HUMOUR, ORATION AND FERVOR.
  4. PARTICIPANTS ARE REQUIRED TO PROVIDE DETAILS OF THE ENACTMENT TO THE JUDGES BEFORE THEIR PERFORMANCE.
  5. NO OBSCENE GESTURES,REFERENCES OR LANGUAGES WILL BE ENTERTAINED.
  6. THE MODE OF LANGUAGE CAN BE ENGLISH, HINDI OR BENGALI.
  7. COSTUMES, IF REQUIRED SHOULD BE APPROPRIATE.
  8. judges' decision will be final and binding
  9. TEAMS WILL BE PENALIZED IF THEY CROSS THE TIME LIMIT.
  10. USAGE OF ANY HEAVY OBJECT, FLAME POWDER IS NOT ALLOWED.
  11. TEAMS MUST PERFORM AN EXTENDED VERSION OF THEIR PERFORMANCE PERFORMED DURING XTACY IN XAVOTSAV.

FREQUENCY 102

RADIO PLAY

THE SHARPEST WEAPON YOU HAVE IS YOUR VOICE. MOULD YOUR VOICE, USE YOUR INNER BEAST TO MAKE A VOICE COME ALIVE AND SHOWCASE A PICTURE. NO VISUAL ONLY ACOUSTIC, NO GESTURE ONLY SOUND EFFECTS. USE YOUR VOICE TO CALL a SPADE A SPADE AND MAKE EVEN THE GREAT SENECA PROUD. DIALOGUE CAN BE REVOLUTIONARY, YOU JUST NEED TO SAY IT RIGHT.

NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS: 1-3 (1 CAS SHALL BE ALLOWED)
THEME: WILL BE INFORMED TO THE CONTINGENT LEADER
STRUCTURE:
THE PARTICIPANT(S) HAVE TO SUBMIT A RADIO PLAY WITHIN THE PRESCRIBED TIME LIMIT BASED ON A GIVEN TOPIC (5 MINUTES [INCLUDING CREDITS]).

rules:

  1. A TEAM WILL SUBMIT A RADIO DRAMA OF NOT MORE THAN 5 MINUTES ON THE THEME.
  2. IT MAY INCLUDE UPTO 6 CHARACTERS.
  3. A TEAM CAN HAVE 2-4 PARTICIPANTS (EXTRA VOICE ACTORS WILL NOT BE COUNTED AS PARTICIPANTS)
  4. THE NAME OF THE RADIO PLAY, THE NAMES OF THE CHARACTERS AND THE PERSON ENACTING THEM MUST BE RECORDED AT THE BEGINNING OF THE PLAY.
  5. LANGUAGES USED CAN BE ENGLISH, BENGALI AND HINDI.
  6. IT MUST BE SUBMITTED IN A PENDRIVE IN MP3 OR WAV FORMAT TO THE ORGANIZERS.
  7. ALL THE PARTICIPANTS OF THE TEAM NEED TO BE PRESENT DURING SCREENING.
  8. ANY SOFTWARE CAN BE USED TO MAKE THE PLAY.
  9. ONE TEAM IS ALLOWED PER COLLEGE.
  10. JUDGEMENT WILL BE DONE ON THE BASIS OF SCRIPT, VOCAL ACTING, SOUND EDITING AND DESIGNING.
  11. NOISELESS AND DISTORTION LESS PRODUCTIONS ARE HIGHLY APPRECIATED.
  12. DECISION OF THE JUDGE(S) WILL BE FINAL AND BINDING

judging criteria:

  • VOCAL ACTING (SYNC, MODULATION, DICTATION)
  • sound editing
  • script
  • overall impact
  • humour
